1. Our Commitment

Maqoola has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and child sexual abuse material (CSAM). We strictly prohibit any content, behavior, or activity that sexualizes, endangers, exploits, or otherwise harms minors on our platform.

2. Prohibited Conduct

The following are strictly prohibited within Maqoola:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind.
  • Grooming, solicitation, or sexual extortion of minors.
  • Sexualized depictions, descriptions, or quotes involving minors.
  • Trafficking, exploitation, or endangerment of children.
  • Sharing personally identifying information (PII) of minors without consent.
  • Any content that violates applicable child safety laws.

3. In-App Reporting Mechanism

Maqoola provides users with an in-app reporting mechanism. Any user can flag content, quotes, comments, or profiles suspected of violating these standards by:

  • Using the in-app Report action on the offending content or user.
  • Emailing ahmedfaragmostafa@gmail.com with subject line CHILD SAFETY REPORT.

Reports are reviewed within 24 hours. Confirmed violations result in immediate content removal and permanent account termination.

4. CSAM Handling Procedure

When CSAM is identified or reported, Maqoola will:

  • Remove the offending content immediately upon confirmation.
  • Permanently ban the responsible account(s).
  • Preserve relevant evidence as required by law.
  • Report confirmed CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and/or applicable local authorities.
  • Cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations.

5. Child Safety Point of Contact

For child safety concerns, law enforcement requests, or CSAE-related inquiries:

  • Name: Ahmad Farag
  • Email: ahmedfaragmostafa@gmail.com
  • Response time: Within 48 hours for non-urgent matters; within 24 hours for urgent child safety reports.

6. Legal Compliance

Maqoola complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations, including:

  • U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).
  • EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and GDPR-K provisions for minors.
  • 18 U.S.C. § 2258A (reporting of CSAM to NCMEC).
  • Google Play's Child Safety Standards Policy and Developer Program Policies.
  • All applicable local child protection laws in jurisdictions where Maqoola is available.

7. Age Requirements

Maqoola is intended for users aged 13 and older. Users under the minimum age required by their local jurisdiction are not permitted to create accounts or use the service.

8. Updates to These Standards

These standards may be updated periodically to reflect changes in law, policy, or platform practices. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
